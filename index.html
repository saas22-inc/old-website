A Note from the Founder

We started this journey in 2024, when it became clear that distribution in tech was breaking.

Inbound marketing was losing effectiveness as buyers began discovering products through LLMs, not blogs. Outbound got saturated with spray-and-pray messaging. SaaS became easier to build but harder to differentiate -- while AI blurred trust across digital and real-world interactions.

Instead of rushing to build yet another product struggling to crack distribution, we chose a different path -- to help solve distribution itself.

Over the last two years, we've worked with 15+ SaaS and AI companies across geographies and verticals. We launched partner programs, built co-sell motions, onboarded hundreds of partners, and ran curated events across continents -- operating deep at the intersection of AI, partnerships, and GTM.

A few things became clear:

  • Traditional inbound and outbound GTM models are losing steam
  • Partnerships fail not because of intent, but because of execution
  • Software alone isn't enough -- outcomes matter
  • Contextual AI can fundamentally reshape partner ecosystems

A New Name
SaaS22 reflected where we started -- during a moment when SaaS itself was being reshaped. But our work has since expanded well beyond a single category.

JazzHQ is the productization of our learnings in the last two years.
We're now building infrastructure for partner-led distribution and ecosystems across SaaS, AI, and traditionally channel-first industries.
JazzHQ better represents this evolution.

JazzHQ is an AI-powered Partner Operating System, backed by forward-deployed teams and a curated marketplace -- built to help companies scale through partners, predictably and globally.

The name reflects how we think about growth today: not as a rigid, linear motion, but as an orchestrated system -- where partners, products, and GTM motions come together in rhythm. It also signals our ambition to be the control center for partner-led growth, not just another point solution.

We remain rooted in SaaS and AI, while actively foraging into a broader spectrum of partner-first industries -- including IT services, manufacturing, real estate, telcos, fintech, and beyond.

We're evolving, but we'll always prioritize outcomes over software.

We're still learning -- yet we're convinced of one thing:

with all else equal, distribution is the only moat, and partnerships are how modern companies win.

FOUNDER AND CEO OF JAZZHQ (FORMERLY SAAS22)
